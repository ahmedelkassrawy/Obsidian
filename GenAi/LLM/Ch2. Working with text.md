Word2Vec trained neural network architecture to generate word embeddings by predicting
the context of a word given the target word or vice versa. The main idea
behind Word2Vec is that words that appear in similar contexts tend to have similar
meanings. Consequently, when projected into two-dimensional word embeddings for
visualization purposes, similar terms are clustered together
